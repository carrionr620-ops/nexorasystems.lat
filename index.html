FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Nexora Systems.

How long does the setup take?
Most clients are fully set up and live within 5–7 business days. We handle the entire installation — you just provide access to your channels and we take it from there.
Do you offer ongoing support?
Yes. Every plan includes an optimization period (30, 60 or 90 days depending on your plan) where we monitor, adjust, and improve the system based on real results.
Which channels does Nexora connect to?
WhatsApp, Instagram DMs, Facebook Messenger, email, and your website contact forms. The Professional plan covers 3 channels and Enterprise covers all of them simultaneously.
Is the AI personalized to my business?
Yes. We train the AI on your specific services, pricing, tone of voice, and frequently asked questions. It responds exactly as your best sales person would — 24 hours a day.
Are there monthly fees after setup?
No. The Nexora System is a one-time investment. There are no recurring platform fees from us. You only pay for your own channel subscriptions if applicable.
What if I need changes after launch?
During your optimization period, any changes are included. After that, we offer affordable update packages. Most clients find the system works so well they rarely need modifications.
